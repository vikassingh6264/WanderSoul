KARNATAKA · HERITAGE & UNESCO · MID-RANGE

The Boulder-Strewn Empire of Hampi

Walk the ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire, where ancient stone temples stand amidst massive granite boulders and lush banana plantations along the Tungabhadra River.

UP-02
UTTAR PRADESH · CULTURAL & SPIRITUAL · BUDGET

Varanasi's Sacred Shores

Witness life and death on the historic stone steps along the sacred Ganges river, where music, incense, and chants echo at dawn.

ML-09
MEGHALAYA · NATURE & TREKKING · MID-RANGE

The Living Root Bridges

Hike down steep valley steps to witness ancient suspension bridges grown organically by the Khasi people from living tree roots.
